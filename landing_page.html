Yes. Your sessions and personal data are stored securely in our database. We never share or sell your data. You can delete your account anytime.

Yes, fully. SpeakSmart works in any mobile browser — Chrome, Safari. Voice input also works on mobile. No app download needed.

You'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro at ₹299/month for unlimited sessions. Your data and history are always saved — upgrading just unlocks more practice.

Yes — ChatGPT is a general tool. SpeakSmart is purpose-built for interviews: structured flow, scoring, live English corrections, Hindi/Hinglish, Indian job categories, and session tracking. None of that exists out of the box.

Yes. Hindi and Hinglish are live now. Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, and more regional languages are on the roadmap. India is a multi-language market and we're building for all of it.
