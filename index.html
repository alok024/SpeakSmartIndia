FAQ

Common questions

Is my data safe?
Yes. Your sessions and personal data are stored securely in our database. We never share or sell your data. You can delete your account anytime.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes, fully. SpeakSmart works in any mobile browser — Chrome, Safari. Voice input also works on mobile. No app download needed.
What happens after my free sessions run out?
You'll be prompted to upgrade. Free gives you 5 sessions/month. Pro gives 60/month at ₹299. Elite gives unlimited at ₹599. All plans have an annual option with ~30% off.
Is this better than practicing with ChatGPT?
Yes — ChatGPT is a general tool. SpeakSmart is purpose-built for interviews: structured flow, scoring, live English corrections, Hindi/Hinglish, Indian job categories, and session tracking. None of that exists out of the box in ChatGPT.
Will more languages be added?
Yes. Hindi and Hinglish are live now. Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, and more regional languages are on the roadmap.
Can I cancel my plan anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime — you keep access till the end of the billing period. Annual plans are prepaid and non-refundable after 7 days.
